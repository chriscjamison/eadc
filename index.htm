Testimonial

Emmanuel is a warm and inviting place. They truly are what they say they are, care and compassion.

Emmanuel Adult Day Center provides a structured program of therapeutic, rehabilitative, social and leisure activities in a well-monitored setting. We currently service individuals who are 18 and over and meet the necessary requirements of the program through state issued guidelines.

EADC is certified by the Missouri Department of Health and Senior Services

Why EADC is good for your senior?

Counseling EADC has on-site Licensed Professional Counselor to meet with both participants and their families to help aid in their mental health needs. Services include psychological/emotional support through individual & group sessions. Case management services are also provided on an as needed basis. Please visit the website of our counselor, Nicole Johnson, to learn more about her services - https://nljcounselingandcoaching.com.

Medical care/Health screenings EADC has an in-house RN to provide medical services & health screenings to each participant. Our RN on roster meets regularly with particpants to complete routine vital checks and administer any medications on-site. The RN also provides direct communication to families and Primary Care Physicians on any changes or concerns of the participant. The RN also coordinates with the PCP any other services provided on-site.

Community EADC creates an optimal environment for community & socialization in our facility. We want our seniors to use their mental faculties to the fullest. We focus our efforts to ensure your loved ones are continually engaged through communication and discussions, arts & crafts, field trips, daily recreation, and much more.
